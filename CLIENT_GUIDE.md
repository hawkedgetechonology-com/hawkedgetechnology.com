# Client User Guide — HawkEdge Version 1.0

This guide documents client workflows from Discovery submission to Studio project tracking.

---

## 🧭 Public Website Flow

1. **Discovery wizard**: Navigate to `https://hawkedge.tech` (or `http://localhost:3000`), complete the Discovery questionnaire, and review your estimated system score.
2. **Consultation booking**: Select an available slot from the calendar slot picker to schedule a meeting with our systems architects.

---

## 🎨 Client Studio Portal

Once a proposal is accepted, you will receive an invitation email containing login credentials. Access the Client Studio at `https://studio.hawkedge.tech` (or `http://localhost:3001` locally):

- **Login Onboarding**: Log in using your email and the one-time temporary password. You will be prompted to reset your password immediately for security compliance.
- **Project Sprints & Milestones**: Monitor active system builds, sprint deliverables, and team ownership assignments.
- **Invoices**: View billed schedules, download invoices, and review payment notes.
- **Support Tickets**: Submit technical queries or bug reports. Support agents review tickets directly in Mission Control.
